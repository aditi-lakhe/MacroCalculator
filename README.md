 MacroCalculator
Python project that calculates macros based on physical factors and activity level , and suggests food based on requirements..
<br>

📊 How it Works
1. Input Profile: Enter your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level in the sidebar.
2. Set Goals: Choose whether you want to lose, maintain, or gain weight.
3. Generate Plan: The app calculates your daily "Targets" for Calories, Carbs, Protein, and Fats.
4. Get Recommendations: Browse a curated list of Indian dishes that fit your specific caloric budget for the day.
